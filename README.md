# Python-Developer-Survey-Clustering

The dataset is from kaggle "Stack Overflow 2018 Developer Survey"(https://www.kaggle.com/stackoverflow/stack-overflow-2018-developer-survey/kernels)
Datset consists of 98,855 responses in 129 columns. Each row represents a survey response by a developers. 

Managerial Insights
For freshers, if you do master's you can earn higher than your colleagues at the start of your career. However, in long run your experience determines your salaries.
For working professional with more than 9 years of experience, there is no advantage in terms of salaries. So, for these people doing master's is not desirable in orde to increase their salary.
